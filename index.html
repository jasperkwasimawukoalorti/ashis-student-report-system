🗄️ Archive Academic Year

Save a complete snapshot of the current academic year — all students, scores, and records — before starting a new year.

ℹ️ Recommended: Always archive before promoting students. The archived file can be re-imported later for reference.

🎓 Promote Students

Advance students to the next form at the end of the academic year. Form 3 students will be graduated and removed from the system.

⚠️ Important: This action moves all students up one form. Form 1 → Form 2, Form 2 → Form 3, Form 3 → Graduated. All scores are cleared for the new year. This cannot be undone — take a backup first!

🗑️ Clear All Data

Permanently delete all data from the system. This action cannot be undone!

⚠️ DANGER ZONE: This will delete all students, teachers, scores, and settings permanently!
